Bake-kit

• scales/measuring cup

• measuring spoons

• mixing bowl

Time

• 15 m. prep

• 10 m. baking

Yield

24 noms

Cost

2 out of 5 monies

Difficulty

1 out of 5 biscuits

How to biscuit

1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C

2. Rough chop the macadamia nuts, and tip into a mixing bowl.

3. To the nuts, add the flour, sugar, oats bicarbonate of soda and raspberries and stir to together to combine.

4. Melt the butter (microwave 30 seconds) and mix in the golden syrup (Add the vanilla now if you want to include.

5. Pour the butter and syrup into the dry mixture and stir with a spoon. Make little walnut sized balls and place on a baking tray.

6. Bake in the oven for 10 minutes, or until pale golden-brown. Cool on a wire rack.

What to biscuit

• 150g self raising flour

• 150g caster sugar

• 120g rolled oats

• 1 level teaspoon bicarbonate of soda

• 150g butter

• 2 tbsp golden syrup

• 50g macadamia nuts

• 100g white chocolate chips

• Freeze dried raspberries

• vanilla (optional)
