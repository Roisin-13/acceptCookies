Bake-kit

• scales/measuring cup

• mixing bowl

• hand mixer

• rolling pin

• cling film

• cookie cutter

Time

• 20 m. prep

• 40 m. chilling

• 10 m. baking

Yield

24 noms

Cost

2 out of 5 monies

Difficulty

2 out of 5 biscuits

How to biscuit

1.Preheat the oven to 190°C

2.Beat the butter and the sugar together with a handheld mixer until the mixture is smooth.

3.Using the mixer, stir in the flour, and cornflour – and vanilla if using it – until the mix just starts to come together. Be careful not to over mix.

3.Sprinkle in the freeze dried strawberry pieces, and use the egg yolk to bind the mixture together.

4. Tip the dough out onto cling film, and use it to gather the dough together. Make a flat disc, and put into the fridge to chill for 20 minutes.

5.Turn on to a floured work surface and gently roll out until the dough is between 5 and 10 mm in thickness.

6.Use the cookie cutter to cut out heart shapes, and chill in the fridge for a further 20 minutes. Use flat plates to avoid the biscuits bending and separate with cling film if stacking on top of each other so they won’t stick together.

7. Bake in the oven for 10 minutes, then turn down oven to just under 100°C, and cook for a further 5 minutes. Cool on a wire rack.

What to biscuit

• 175 g plain flour

• 50g cornflour

• Vanilla bean paste

• Handful of freeze dried strawberry pieces

• 75 g caster sugar

• 150 g soft butter

• 1 large egg yolk
