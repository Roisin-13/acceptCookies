Bake-kit

• scales/measuring cup

• mixing bowls

• hand mixer

• spatula

• baking pan

• piping bags & nozzle

Time

• 20 m. prep

• 40 m. baking

Yield

10 noms

Cost

1 out of 5 monies

Difficulty

1 out of 5 cake

How to cook

1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C

2. Beat butter, sugar and lemon zest until fluffy.

3. Beat in eggs, one at a time, until incorporated.

4. Sift flour onto mix, adding lemon juice, and lemon essence to loosen mixture.

5. Line a large square baking tray, and pour in the batter.

6. Bake for for 35-40 mins, or when poked with a knife, it comes out clean.

7. Cool on a rack, while cooling make the glace icing: mix icing sugar with water, and lemon essence until thick and smooth. Take ¼ out for stripe decoration.

8. Take the ¾ of the glace icing, and spread over the cake.

9. With the ¼ that has been reserved, add the yellow colour and zig zag over the cake.

What to use

• 250g soft unsalted butter

• 350g sugar

• 4 eggs

• 350g flour

• Zest and juice of 2 lemons

• 1 tsp lemon essence

• Glace Icing

• 250g icing sugar

• 2 tbsp warm water

• Yellow food colouring

• ½ tsp lemon essence
