Bake-kit

• scales/measuring cup

• mixing bowls

• hand mixer

• cupcake cases

• muffin pan

• icecream scoop/spoon

Time

• 20 m. prep

• 20 m. baking

Yield

14 noms

Cost

1 out of 5 monies

Difficulty

1 out of 5 cake

How to cook

1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C

2. Sift together the flour, cocoa powder and baking powder, then stir in the both sugars, making sure to break up any lumps.

3. Pour in both of the chocolate chips.

4. Put the milk, oil, and eggs in a jug, and whisk.

5. Mix the liquid ingredients into the dry by hand, with minimal stirring.

6. Line the cupcake tray, and fill cases, up to nearly the top.

7. Bake for 20 mins or until springy to the touch.

8. Take muffins out of tray, and cool on a wire rack.

What to use

• 250g self-raising flour

• 25g cocoa powder

• 2tsp baking powder

• 125g golden caster sugar and 50g muscovado sugar

• 175ml milk

• 2 large eggs, beaten

• 100ml sunflower oil

• 100g dark chocolate chips

• 100g white chocolate chips
