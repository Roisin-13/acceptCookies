Bake-kit

• scales/measuring cup

• mixing bowls

• hand mixer

• spatula

• baking pan

Time

• 20 m. prep

• 25 m. baking

Yield

24 noms

Cost

2 out of 5 monies

Difficulty

2 out of 5 cake

How to cook

1. Pre-heat the oven to 160°C

2. Break the chocolate up, and cube the butter, put it all in a bowl. Melt the butter and chocolate the microwave, until liquid, then leave to cool.

3. Sieve flour, and cocoa powder into a separate bowl, and mix.

4. Put eggs and sugar in a large bowl and beat, for a few minutes. Beat until it doubles in size, and gets pale.

5. Take a spatula, and gently fold in the chocolate butter until the mixture is all the same colour (try not to knock out too much air from the mixture).

6. Sieve flour mix into the wet mixture, add the chocolate chips and using the spatula again, gently fold until no dry spots are left. Don't overwork.

7. Line the baking tray with baking paper, or tinfoil, and pour mixture in.

8. Bake for 25 minutes. If still wobbly in middle, put in for another 5 minutes.

9. Cool in the baking tray, then cut into squares.

What to use

• 200g soft unsalted butter

• 200g dark chocolate

• 50g cocoa powder

• 90g plain flour

• 3 large eggs

• 275g golden caster sugar

• 100g chocolate chips
