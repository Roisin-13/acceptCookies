Bake-kit

• scales/measuring cup

• measuring spoons

• mixing bowl

• hand mixer

• cupcake cases

• muffin pan

• icecream scoop/spoon

• piping bags & nozzle

Time

• 20 m. prep

• 30 m. decorating

• 25 m. baking

Yield

12 noms

Cost

3 out of 5 monies

Difficulty

2 out of 5 cake

How to cupcake

1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C

2. Beat butter, sugar and vanilla essence until fluffy.

3. In a seperate bowl, crush the mangos, until smooth, then add to the butter and sugar.

4. Beat in eggs, one at a time, until incorporated.

5. Sift flour, and baking powder onto mix, adding milk a spoon at a time.

6. Add chocolate chips, and briefly mix.

7. Line the muffin tray with cupcake cases, and using the icecream scoop, or spoon, divide the batter, between them (do not fill more than 2/3 full).

8. Bake the cakes for 20-25 minutes, or until when it is poked with a knife it comes out clean.

9. While the cakes are cooling, make the buttercream. Melt the chocolate in the microwave in 10 second bursts, and leave to cool.

10. Beat the butter, and icing sugar until fluffy, and add the melted white chocolate, add the milk to loosen the mixture, add the flavour, and the colour (to your desired shade).

11. Make sure the cakes are cool, and pipe the buttercream on, and decorate with sprinkles (if using).

What to cupcake

• 125g soft unsalted butter

• 175g self-raising flour

• 175g caster sugar

• 2 large eggs

• Half a can of mangoes (200g)

• 100g white chocolate chips

• ½ tsp baking powder

• 6 tbsp milk

Buttercream

• 150g butter

• 200g chocolate

• 350g icing sugar

• 1tsp mango flavour

• 1-2 tbsp milk

• Orange food colouring

Decoration

• Sprinkles (optional)
