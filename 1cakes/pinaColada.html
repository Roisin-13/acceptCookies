Bake-kit

• scales/measuring cup

• measuring spoons

• mixing bowl

• hand mixer

• cupcake cases

• muffin pan

• icecream scoop/spoon

• piping bags & nozzle

• fondant modelling kit

Time

• 20 m. prep

• 1 hr. decorating

• 25 m. baking

Yield

12 noms

Cost

5 out of 5 monies

Difficulty

4 out of 5 cake

How to cupcake

1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C

2. Beat butter, sugar and pineapple jam until fluffy.

3. Beat in eggs, one at a time, until incorporated.

4. Sift flour onto mix, adding rum a spoon at a time.

5. Line the muffin tray with cupcake cases, and using the icecream scoop, or spoon, divide the batter, between them (do not fill more than 2/3 full).

6. Bake the cakes for 20-25 minutes, or until when it is poked with a knife it comes out clean.

7. While the cakes are cooling, if you want a stronger flavour, drizzle with extra rum.

8. Make the buttercream. start by melting the chocolate in the microwave in 10 second bursts, and leave to cool.

9. Beat the butter, and icing sugar until fluffy, and add the melted white chocolate, add the malibu to loosen the mixture, and add the colour (to your desired shade).

10. Make sure the cakes are cool, and pipe the buttercream on, and if you can, model little flowers out of the fondant, and decorate by placing the flowers on the side, and topping with a Maraschino cherry.

What to cupcake

• 125g soft unsalted butter

• 175g self-raising flour

• 175g caster sugar

• 2 large eggs

• 4 tbsp pineapple jam

• 6 tbsp dark rum (or more as needed)

Buttercream

• 150g butter

• 200g chocolate

• 350g icing sugar

• 1-2 tbsp malibu

• Yellow food colouring

Decoration

• Stalk on Maraschino cherries

• Fondant icing
