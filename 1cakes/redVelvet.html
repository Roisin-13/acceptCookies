Bake-kit

• scales/measuring cup

• measuring spoons

• mixing bowls

• hand mixer

• cupcake cases

• muffin pan

• icecream scoop/spoon

• piping bags & nozzle

Time

• 45 m. prep

• 30 m. decorating

• 25 m. baking

Yield

12 noms

Cost

3 out of 5 monies

Difficulty

4 out of 5 cake

How to cupcake

1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C

2. Sift together the flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt.

3. Beat butter, and sugar light and fluffy.

4. Beat in eggs, one at a time, until incorporated.

5. Mix buttermilk/kefir, vanilla and red colouring to give a bright red colour. Add a third of the dry ingredients to the butter mix and fold in, followed by half the buttermilk, and repeat until it’s all used up.

6. Use 4 tbsp milk to rinse out kefir and pour in.

7. Mix ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda and vinegar together and add to the mixture, then sprinkle ¼ tsp on top, and fold in.

8. Line the muffin tray with cupcake cases, and using the icecream scoop, or spoon, divide the batter, between them (do not fill more than ⅔ full).

9. Bake the cakes for 20-25 minutes, or until when it is poked with a knife it comes out clean.

10. While the cakes are cooling, if you want a stronger flavour, drizzle with extra rum.

11. Make the buttercream. start by melting the chocolate in the microwave in 10 second bursts, and leave to cool.

12. Beat the butter, and icing sugar until fluffy, and add the melted white chocolate, squeeze as much liquid as you can out of the cream cheese, and mix in.

10. Make sure the cakes are cool, and pipe the buttercream on, add sprinkles, and top with a Maraschino cherry.

What to cupcake

• 175g soft unsalted butter

• 225g plain flour

• 2½ tsp cocoa powder

• ½ tsp baking powder

• ½ salt

• 175g caster sugar

• 2 large eggs, beaten

• 155ml buttermilk/kefir

• 4 tbsp milk

• Vanilla essence

• Red food colouring

• ¾ tsp bicarbonate of soda

• 1tbsp cider vinegar

Buttercream

• 150g butter

• 200g chocolate

• 350g icing sugar

• ½ tub cream cheese (100g)

Decoration

• Stalk on Maraschino cherries

• Heart sprinkles
